About Us




About PetNest

PetCare is your one-stop destination for all your pet's needs. Whether you have a playful pup, a curious kitten, or a chirping companion, we offer a wide range of high-quality pet food, accessories, grooming supplies, and expert advice.



Who We Are

We are a passionate team of animal lovers dedicated to making every pet’s life better.

At PetNest, we believe every pet deserves love, care, and access to the best products. Since 2020, we’ve been helping pet parents across Sri Lanka find quality food, accessories, grooming tools, and more – all under one roof.


Our Mission

To ensure every pet in Sri Lanka enjoys a healthy, happy life by offering the highest-quality products and personalized service to pet parents everywhere.
